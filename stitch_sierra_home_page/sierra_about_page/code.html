Executive Profile

Architecting the Future of African Tech.

As the CTO of Africentric Technology Hub, Michael Ibangha operates at the vital intersection of high-level business strategy and deep-core engineering. With over a decade of experience navigating the complex digital landscapes of the continent, he has become a pivotal figure in driving large-scale digital transformation.

Michael’s leadership philosophy is built on the belief that technology should be "Africentric"—innovated locally but engineered to compete globally. He leads multidisciplinary teams in developing scalable SaaS ecosystems, mobile-first banking solutions, and intuitive user experiences that bridge the gap between technical complexity and human connection.

His tenure has seen the successful deployment of over 100+ high-impact projects, helping brands evolve from legacy systems to modern, cloud-native architectures.
